ABOUT US

SOLUTIONS YOU CAN IMPLMENENT.
Our team of experts are backed by on-the-ground experience, and close industry partnerships to ensure we deliver solutions you can implement. We service hard-rock mining companies with our expertise in mining engineering, geology and geotechnical.
OUR STORY
IN THE BEGINNING
Mining Plus was founded in 2006 in Perth, Western Australia. The company was started by two mining engineers, Ben Auld and Peter Lock, who saw an opportunity to provide innovative and pragmatic consulting services to the mining industry. Peter and Ben both previously worked for large mining companies and had extensive experience in mining operations and project management. They realised that many mining companies lacked the capacity to successfully plan and execute mining projects. They saw an opportunity to provide this expertise as an independent consultancy firm. Initially, Mining Plus focused on providing mining engineering services, such as mine planning and design, feasibility studies, and project management. As the company grew, it expanded its services to include geology, geotechnical engineering, and investment advisory services. Today, Mining Plus has grown into a global company with offices in Australia, Canada, and US. The company has a team of over 150+ mining professionals who provide a wide range of consulting services to mining companies worldwide.
