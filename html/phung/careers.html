Driving passion & excellence in mining.

We invest in our team and foster an 'A-game' mindset where our collective success is harnessed by the technical excellence and personal development of each team member. We are strong advocates for the mining industry and the important role it plays in modern society. We therefore are proactive in our support of the future generation of mining professionals who will be the driving force in meaningful promotion of the resources industry.

Implementing cutting-edge technology.

Mining Plus has a robust technology intelligence program and hands-on experience in implementing the new technologies at mine sites of top producers. This program is built upon both our unique intellectual properties and proactive engagement with METS companies.

Our CULTURE

Passion & Excellence in Mining.

At Mining Plus, we foster an environment of collaboration, innovation, and continuous growth, where every team member is valued and empowered to make a difference. We are fuelled by a deep-rooted love for the mining industry and a relentless pursuit of excellence in everything we do. This passion sparks our curiosity and motivates us to continuously learn, innovate, and push the boundaries of what is possible in mining. We set high standards and hold ourselves accountable to them.

Delivering top-quality design and services globally.

We bring global solutions to local challenges. Innovation and results are not constrained by the location of our team members - We work as 'One Team' across the globe to leverage a rich technical perspective to deliver highly practical solutions.

Our global reach extends into our partner groups at Byrnecut- Thyssen Mining Group who are specialist underground mining contractors employing more than 10,000 people collectively.
