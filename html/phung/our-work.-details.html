Overview

Mining Plus was engaged to assess the feasibility of mining a deeper ore zone within the Client’s existing underground lead-zinc mine in Australia. Building on a 2019 Desktop Study, the Mining Concept Study evaluated methods to extend the mine’s life by exploiting this lower orebody through optimized mining techniques, materials handling, and scheduling.

Challenges

The deeper ore zone presented several challenges, including determining the optimal mining method for varying orebody widths, establishing an efficient materials handling system, and maintaining a steady production rate. Trade-offs were required to assess the suitability of switching from transverse to longitudinal stoping in narrower ore zones and to explore the best layout for ore/waste passes and crusher bins. Additionally, there was a need to minimize production disruptions while integrating deeper level mining, and project risks had to be thoroughly assessed given the limited geological and geotechnical data for the area.

Our Solutions

Mining Plus evaluated multiple design options for both mining method and materials handling. The study confirmed that bottom-up transverse stoping with paste backfill is optimal for wider areas, while longitudinal stoping is more suitable for narrower sections. Material handling was optimized by positioning ore pass systems and crusher bins in ways that minimize haulage distances and allow for simultaneous ore flow from upper and lower levels. Schedule scenarios using a single retreat chevron and a sill pillar were modeled to determine a steady production profile of 850–900 kt/yr. The preferred design was selected for financial analysis to ensure commercial viability.

Outcomes

The Mining Concept Study provided a clear pathway for extending the life of the existing mine by accessing deeper ore zones. The selected mining methods and materials handling configurations are expected to achieve a steady production rate of 850–900 kt/yr. By optimizing stope layout, level spacing, and ore pass configurations, Mining Plus helped mitigate logistical challenges and improve operational efficiency. The final report identified key risks and forward work plans, enabling the Client to confidently advance the project to the next study phase with a foundation for sustained, economically viable production.
